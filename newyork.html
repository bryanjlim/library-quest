New York Library Quest 📚

New York has 23 public library systems, each maintaining its own independent Libby/OverDrive collection. Every card you collect unlocks a different catalog of ebooks and audiobooks.

Pro tip: ANY New York State resident can get free digital library cards at The New York Public Library (NYPL), Brooklyn Public Library, and Queens Public Library — three of the largest Libby collections in the country — regardless of which county you live in.

Dot color = system type. Consolidated (NYC systems), Federated (county-led), or Cooperative (member-library associations). Each system has its own independent Libby catalog.
